about us

IndoGlobal Institute of management and training (IIMT) was formed on 6th May 2007 for the student community around the world with a mission of imparting professional and management education in order to fullfill the growing need of the global business, industry and commerce for the effective managers, intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs. The institute functions under the Aegis of Jagraj Educational and welfare society. The academy and the advisory board of the institute comprises of renowned academician coorporate personality and practitior from the different sectors of our economy. The main objective is to assimilate and inculcate managerial skills and traits among the students propelling them to become effective managers for Global Business. Industry and commerce.
