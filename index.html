about us

IndoGlobal Institute of management and training (IIMT) was for formed on 6 May 2007 for the student community around the world with an mission of imparting professional and management education in order to fullfill the growing need of the global business, industry and commerce for the effective manager,intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs.The institute function under the Aegis of Jagraj Educational and welfare society. The academy and the advisory board of the institute comprises of renouned academician coorporate personality and practitior from the different sector of our economy. The main objective is to assimilate and inculcate managerial skills and traits among the students propelling them to become effective managers for Global Business. Industry and commerce.

Training Methodology

Our approach towards training is simple but effective.Our aim is to help students achieve their potential by ensuring that they are taught clearly and effectively so as to facilate succeess in the course assessments and examinations- which eventually lead to success in their carreers.Here are the modes of the delivery are

Seminars

It complements lectures and are designed to test the understanding of the material covered in the lectures and private study by reference material usually to a set of questions identified by the tutor/facilitator in advance.Seminars are the interactive session that student prepare. They give students an oppertunity to clarify , points of difficulty if any with the tutor/facilitator and also develop their oral communication skills.

Lectures

It is primarily intended to identify and explian key aspect of the subject so that student can utilize thier private study time more effectively.They are student guides and provide a focus route through syllabus. Our lectures ethos is to guide , explain and teach rather than to transmit factual material which can be readily obtained from the recommended texts in the private study.

Case Study

An important teaching methodology used at IIMT is use of the case studies. Case studies enable students to put into use all the concepts they learn in their subjects.The students would have to study the case, analyze the fact presented therien, and arrive at conclusions and recommendations. The case study will test the student ability to apply, to the real world, the tools and the technique of the analysis which they have heard.

Workshop

They are the innovation form of the student centered learning.They involve student working in the small groups with a difference- student leads and the tutor act as the facilitator. The session consider problems, eassy questions and case studies and tackle the conceptually aspects of the subjects.
